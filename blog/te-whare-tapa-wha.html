Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Drink at least a litre of water per day, ski once a week or gym twice a week.
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) Plan a date day or activity with my partner once a week. Calling my parents once a month would also be a great goal.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Sit outside on the picnic blanket with a coffee and play with my cat once a week. Pick up rubbish when I see it on the ground.
