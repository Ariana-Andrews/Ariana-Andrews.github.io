Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

When people understand the principles of neuroplasticity, it helps them understand that intelligence can be developed. It can also help them create better learning environments. The task at hand should have learning material that is targeted, significant to the objective, relevant and ideally individualized. The material should be repeated in different ways to strengthen the brain's connections and memory. The material should be engaging, which is important for neuroplasticity. Improving material to help learn may look like creating multisensory tasks, learning through play, and storytelling to help deepen the students' connection to the material.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit.

I will focus on one learning objective at a time and reduce other distractions whilst learning a new skill. I will also try to come back to old learning material and practice it regularly.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

The following activities should be done regularly to increase your brain's neuroplasticity:

  • Learn new skills
  • Exercise/ do physical activity
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet
  • Get good rest and sleep
  • Manage your stress levels and decrease cortisol
  • Engage in social activities

Discuss what it is Growth Mindset and why it is relevant.

It is a mindset that helps enable people to push through hard learning or challenging times. It is a perspective that often correlates to not being afraid of failure or asking for help. In relation to learning how to code, this mindset may help students to push through difficult coding challenges, whether it’s asking for help, or giving it a try even if they don’t understand how to solve the problem right away.
