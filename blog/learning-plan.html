What are your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths

  • I am an extremely fast visual learner (videos, images, live demonstrations).
  • I am aware of when I have resistance to certain tasks or learning objectives. This allows me to stop and reflect on what exactly is causing the resistance, and try to find ways to overcome it.
  • I push through difficult tasks or learning objectives, even if it takes a long time, to fully understand things that confuse me.
  • I’m getting better at knowing when I’m becoming too frustrated and need to step away to ground myself and be in a better mindspace to problem solve effectively.
  • I’m constantly improving my understanding of how I learn and which techniques keep me focused for longer.
  • I am getting better at being kind to myself and knowing when to take breaks and when to push on.

Weaknesses

  • I second guess myself a lot.
  • I can find it hard to express myself/ articulate my thoughts in a written context.
  • I am much slower at learning new skills from written content.
  • I can find it difficult to know which task is more important to prioritize.

This should include: An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you
