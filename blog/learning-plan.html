Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

For my coding career to support my personal hobbies and interests, both financially and to supplement my creative skills. Career pathway wise, I am staying open to ideas and continuing to learn what options are/ could become available to me if I wish to work towards them.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop a fundamental understanding of how to set up a website, front and backend. The ability to set one up that acts as an online store would be a bonus. Human Skills wise, I would like to build a better tool kit for conflict resolution in the workplace.

What are your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths

I am aware of when I have resistance to certain tasks or learning objectives. This allows me to stop and reflect on what exactly is causing the resistance, and try to find ways to overcome it.

I push through difficult tasks or learning objectives, even if it takes a long time, to fully understand things that confuse me.

I’m getting better at knowing when I’m becoming too frustrated and need to step away to ground myself and be in a better mindspace to problem solve effectively.

I’m constantly improving my understanding of how I learn and which techniques keep me focused for longer.

Being kind to myself and knowing when to take breaks and when to push on.

Weaknesses

I struggle to comprehend/ absorb certain types of learning content, i.e written content.

I also struggle with written assignments, and probably take twice as long as the average person to complete simple tasks… like this one…

I can struggle with discipline. Keeping a strict learning schedule is very difficult and currently I find setting time goals, and tracking how much time I actually study for, is better than saying I have to study from x-y. However, this might not always work for some situations/ work loads. I also struggle to switch tasks, especially if I am very interested or locked into the task that I am currently doing. I do prioritize studying and work, however I find that the lack of structure, although it keeps me in a calmer state (which I find better for absorbing information) does mean that I eat into my personal time. It’s a trade off that I am still working through.
